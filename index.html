FAQ

Answers for teams modernising operations.

Platform is an intelligent, modular infrastructure system that combines AI and automation to help teams build, scale, and optimize their software. The neural core keeps learning from live conditions and reacts automatically.

The system watches performance, traffic, and operational signals in real time, then uses predictive models and policy guardrails to adjust routing, capacity, and security posture with minimal human intervention.

Initial deployment usually takes minutes, not weeks. The discovery phase maps your stack quickly, and the first operational recommendations appear immediately after connection.

All plans include guided onboarding. Higher tiers add 24/7 support, dedicated success coverage, and custom incident workflows built around your team.

Pricing is modular. You choose the operational surface you need today, then scale capacity, seats, and enterprise controls without changing the foundation.

Security is built into every module: encrypted traffic, policy-based automation, anomaly detection, and audit-ready workflows. Enterprise deployments can run in private infrastructure.
